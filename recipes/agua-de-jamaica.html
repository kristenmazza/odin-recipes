Agua de Jamaica - Hibiscus Flower Water

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dried hibiscus flowers
  • 2 cups of water
    • Sugar to taste
  • Ice to taste
  • 1/2 gallon of water

Instructions

  1. Boil the dried flowers in the 2 cups of water for 10 minutes. Let it cool. Strain the flowers from the water.
